I am a Ph.D student in the Department of Psychology at Stanford University. I work with Michael Frank in the Language and Cognition Lab studying the development of early word learning and social cognition.

Before grad school, I worked with Anne Fernald investigating the relationship between early language skills and language input. I also helped develop new linguistic processing efficiency measures for children learning American Sign Language. I did my undergrad at Wesleyan University where I worked with Hilary Barth.

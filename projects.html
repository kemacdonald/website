Research interests

Learning is a fundamentally active and social process. How do people select what to learn? And how does the social context affect learning? Currently, I have been exploring these questions in the domain of early language acquisition, using behavioral experiments. I am also interested in the interaction between different types of learning contexts: e.g., observational, communicative, and active.

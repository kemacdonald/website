Research interests

Much of learning occurs in a social-communicative context. How do learners use this information? Currently, I have been exploring this question in the domain of early word learning, using behavioral experiments and computational models. I hope to formalize our intuitions about how social information influences learning. I am also interested in understanding the inferences that learners make in different learning contexts - e.g., with or without pedagogy.
